Masala Puri - Spicy, Flavorful, and Irresistible!

Masala Puri is a beloved street food in India, known for its spicy and tangy flavors. It's a delicious snack where puris are topped with a spicy masala, tamarind chutney, and garnishes.

Ingredients

  • Puris
  • Peas
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sev
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Coriander leaves, chopped
  • Chaat masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the spicy peas masala.
  2. Arrange puris on a plate and create a small opening in the center of each.
  3. Fill each puri with the peas masala.
  4. Top with tamarind chutney, onion, sev, and coriander leaves.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt to taste.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
